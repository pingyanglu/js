About Us
深圳平安讯科技术有限公司，前身为中国平安集团内部孵化的互联网项目， 2017年3月1日获得光启集团5.5亿元融资，并正式独立为平安集团控股公司。 Shenzhen Ping An Xunke Technology Co., Ltd is officially established on March 1st, 2017 and is now a holding company of China Ping An Group. Our internet project was incubated by the parent company, China Ping An Group and successfully received 550 million financing from Kuang-Chi Group.
